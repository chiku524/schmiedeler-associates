About us

Schmiedeler & Associates Inc. has been a trusted partner in the electronics supply chain for over three decades.

We represent overseas customers in the United States, acting as your local purchase agent and cargo consolidator. We serve factories and resellers with reliable access to electronic components and professional equipment from leading brands.

Our experience and relationships across the industry allow us to source, consolidate, and deliver components and equipment efficiently—so you can focus on your operations.
